Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Bangladesh excels in innovation through ICT, microfinance, agritech, and renewable energy.

Sustainability

Bangladesh prioritizes sustainability through climate resilience, renewable energy, and eco-initiatives.

Infrastructure

Bangladesh develops infrastructure with bridges, metro rail, highways, and connectivity.

Empowerment

Bangladesh empowers through education, gender equality, entrepreneurship, and inclusive development.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh envisions a prosperous future driven by sustainable development, innovation, digital transformation,
poverty reduction, climate resilience, inclusive growth, and global leadership in economic and technological advancements.

Professor Muhammad Yunus envisions a transformative future for Bangladesh, where poverty is eradicated through innovation, social business, and microfinance. He emphasizes the importance of education, gender equality, and sustainable development to empower communities and foster economic resilience. Yunus highlights the need for addressing climate challenges while leveraging Bangladesh's potential to emerge as a global leader in inclusive growth, innovation, and social impact, creating a prosperous and equitable nation for all.
